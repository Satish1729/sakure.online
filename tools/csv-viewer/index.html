Frequently Asked Questions

Is my CSV data uploaded to a server?

No. This tool processes your CSV entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your data never leaves your device — there are no server requests, no storage, and no logging of your file contents. It's completely private.

What delimiters are supported?

This viewer supports comma (,), tab, semicolon (;) and pipe (|) delimiters. TSV (tab-separated values) files are auto-detected. You can manually select the correct delimiter from the options bar if auto-detection picks the wrong one.

How many rows can the CSV viewer handle?

The viewer paginates data in batches of 100 rows, so it can comfortably display files with tens of thousands of rows. Very large files (100,000+ rows) may take a few seconds to parse depending on your device's performance.

Can I sort and filter the data?

Yes. Click any column header to sort ascending or descending. Use the search box to filter rows — it searches across all column values simultaneously. Sorting and filtering work together.
