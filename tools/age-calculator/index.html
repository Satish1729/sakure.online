Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate my exact age?+
Enter your date of birth above. The calculator computes the precise difference between your birthday and today (or any chosen date) broken down into years, months, days, hours, and live seconds.
What is the Age Difference calculator for?+
Use the "Age Difference" tab to find the exact gap between two people — useful for siblings, couples, or comparing birthdays. It shows the difference in years, months, days, and total days apart.
Can I find my age on a past or future date?+
Yes! Use the "Age on Date" tab or change the "Age At Date" in My Age tab to any past or future date to calculate your age at that specific time.
How does the live seconds counter work?+
Once you enter your date of birth, the counter calculates total seconds from your birth date to right now and increments every second in real-time using JavaScript's setInterval.
