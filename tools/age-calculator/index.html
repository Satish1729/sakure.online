Leap Year Ages

If you were born on Feb 29, you officially celebrate on Feb 28 or March 1 in non-leap years, depending on convention.

🌍

Age by Country

In South Korea, everyone gains one year on January 1st. In some cultures, age is counted from conception, not birth.

Fun Fact

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate my exact age? +
Enter your date of birth in the calculator above. It computes the difference between today and your birthday in years, months, days, hours, and even seconds.
What is the age difference calculator for? +
Use the "Age Difference" tab to find out the age gap between two people — useful for siblings, couples, or comparing ages. It shows the difference in years and total days.
Can I calculate my age on a future date? +
Yes! Use the "Age on Date" tab or change the "Age At Date" field to any past or future date to see what your age will be (or was) at that time.
