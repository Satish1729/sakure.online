Frequently Asked Questions

How much should I tip at a restaurant in India?+
In India, 10% is a standard restaurant tip. For fine dining or exceptional service, 15–20% is appreciated. Many people also round up the bill. There's no strict rule — tip what feels right for the service quality.
Should I tip on the pre-tax or post-tax amount?+
Most people tip on the pre-tax (subtotal) amount. In India, GST is already included in restaurant bills, so tipping on the total bill is the norm. This calculator uses whichever amount you enter.
How do I split the bill fairly?+
Enter the total bill, choose a tip %, set the number of people, and this calculator divides everything equally. For unequal splits (someone ordered more), calculate each person's sub-total separately.
What does the rounding option do?+
Rounding adjusts the total to the nearest round number (₹5, ₹10, ₹50, or ₹100) to make paying easier. The extra amount goes to the tip, slightly increasing the tip percentage.
