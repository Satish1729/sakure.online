Stacked Discounts

Two discounts don't add up! 20% + 10% off ≠ 30% off. The actual discount is 1 − (0.8 × 0.9) = 28% off.

🛒

Flash Sales

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate the discount price? +
Final Price = Original Price × (1 − Discount%/100). For 25% off ₹2000: 2000 × 0.75 = ₹1500. Use the calculator above for instant results.
How do I find the original price from a sale price? +
Original Price = Sale Price / (1 − Discount%/100). If you see ₹1500 after 25% off: 1500 / 0.75 = ₹2000 original price.
How are stacked discounts calculated? +
Apply each discount sequentially. 20% off then 10% off on ₹1000: After first: ₹800. After second: ₹720. Effective discount = 28%, NOT 30%. They never simply add up.
What does "flat ₹X off" vs "X% off" mean? +
Flat amount off (₹200 off) is always the same regardless of price. Percentage off (20% off) varies with price — better for expensive items. On a ₹500 item, 20% off = ₹100 saved; on ₹2000, 20% = ₹400 saved.
