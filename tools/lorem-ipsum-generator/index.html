Free Lorem Ipsum Placeholder Text Generator

Lorem Ipsum is the standard dummy text used in the printing and typesetting industry since the 1500s. It provides a natural-looking distribution of letters and words that mimics real text, making it ideal for layout and design work where actual content isn't yet available.

Why Use Lorem Ipsum?

When designing websites, apps, or print layouts, using readable English text as placeholder is distracting — reviewers focus on the words instead of the design. Lorem Ipsum solves this by providing text that looks like natural language but doesn't carry meaning, allowing designers and clients to evaluate layout, typography, and spacing without content bias.
